形与色|SHAPE & COLOR

形与色的相互交融一直在这个课程中伴随着我，这些雕塑表面的色彩是我的一种主观审美感知。它们表面的肌理与色彩已成为我这一系列雕塑作品意义表达的重要语言，这种用刻刀所产生的表面效果一定程度上增强了这些雕塑的视觉审美效果和情感表达。
The blend of shape and color has always been with me in this course. The color on the surface of these sculptures is my subjective aesthetic perception. The texture and color of their surface have become an important language for the meaning expression of my series of sculpture works. The surface effect generated by the carving knife enhances the visual aesthetic effect and emotional expression of these sculptures to a certain extent.

————公共雕塑专业 熊峻苓
指导老师 施    海

form, color, painting, sculpture, wild, animal

Sculpture is a high-speed channel through which my consciousness collide with matter, and it is also an external expression of my daily perception. If art has given me a new perspective to observe life, sculpture has given me a unique way to record life.

Sculpting life is the future I see in the vast art field.


雕塑是我所搭建的意识与物质相碰撞的高速通道，也是我对日常感知的一种外在表达。如果说艺术赋予了我全新的视角来观察生活，那么雕塑便赋予了我独特的方式来记录生活。

雕刻生活，是我在广袤无际的艺术领域中看到的未来。
